Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

The Short Version: We collect minimal data. Your documents stay on your computer for local tasks. We don't sell your information. We use standard security practices. Contact us anytime to access, correct, or delete your data.

What We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address: For license delivery, support, and optional updates
  • Payment information: Processed entirely by Stripe — we never see your card details
  • License key: Generated automatically to authenticate your access

Usage Data

  • Task execution logs: Which tasks you run, timestamps, and credit usage
  • Technical logs: API requests, errors, and performance metrics for service operation

What We DON'T Collect

  • Your documents: Local execution tasks process files entirely on your machine — they never reach our servers
  • Business or client details: We don't store the content of your queries beyond what's needed for processing
  • Location tracking: No GPS, no browser fingerprinting
  • Social connections: We are not tracking you across the web

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve the {{PRODUCT_NAME}} service
  • Process payments and manage your credits
  • Send transactional emails (license keys, receipts, support responses)
  • Send optional product updates (if you subscribe)
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraud

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your data. Period.

We share limited data only with essential service providers:

  • Stripe: For payment processing (governed by Stripe's own privacy policy)
  • Google Cloud Platform: Our infrastructure provider for hosting and database services
  • Anthropic: For AI processing of server-side tasks (see Anthropic's data policies for current retention terms)

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

How Tasks Handle Your Data

Server-Side Tasks

Research, lookups, and calculations run on our servers. Your query is sent to us, processed, and results returned. We keep minimal logs for troubleshooting and billing purposes.

Local Execution Tasks

Your documents are never sent to {{PRODUCT_NAME}}. We deliver the expert framework; your local AI assistant applies it to files on your machine. If you use a cloud AI provider, your documents are sent to that provider for processing — review their data policies for details.

Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications

  • Regular security updates

No system is 100% secure. Keep your license key private and use good security practices on your own systems.

Data Retention
