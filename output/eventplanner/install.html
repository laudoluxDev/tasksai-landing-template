What Happens During Install

1
The assistant reads the manifest

It checks agent-install.json in the official EventPlannerTasksAI repo and verifies the repo/domain identity before running anything.

2
The official installer runs

The manifest points to the shared @tasksai/install package with this vertical's product id: eventplanner.

3
You connect your account in the browser

The installer shows a short connection code and opens this site's connect page. Approve with your account email. License-key entry is only a backup.

4
The assistant confirms the install

It runs the health check, tells you whether to restart your MCP client, and confirms your EventPlannerTasksAI credit balance.
